# Reference: 10K Run / Fitness Running App

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Slide 1**: App logo + brand name + 10-week program tag + full-bleed athlete photo background + tilted phone in lower right
- **Green badge headlines**: Short word ("RUN", "GET", "TRACK") in lime green pill/badge — punchy and readable
- **Subtitle below badge**: "With visual and Voice Guidance" in white — describes feature
- **Multi-phone overlapping**: Slide 4 shows 2 phones side by side with overlapping at slight angles
- **White/light card overlays**: Profile cards, social cards overlaid on top of phone screenshots for context

## Color palette
- Background: `#0a0a0a` (dark) or `#f8f8f8` (light alternating)
- Accent: `#7DC832` (lime green)
- Accent pill bg: `rgba(125,200,50,0.15)`
- Text: white on dark, black on light

## Typography
- Headline style: Short keyword in colored pill/badge (`RUN`, `GET`, `TRACK`) + descriptor below
- Descriptor: ~56px, white, regular weight
- Font: Clean sans-serif

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Full bleed photo + tilted phone (partial, bottom-right)
- Slides 2–4: Dark bg + single phone + badge label at top
- Slide 5: Light bg + 2 overlapping phones + friend/social activity overlay card
- Slide 6: Lifestyle photo (runner) partial + feature copy

## Key takeaways
- Short keyword in brand-colored pill works better than a long headline for feature slides
- Overlapping social/friend cards on top of phone shows real usage context
- 2-phone compositions at slight angles show different screens simultaneously
- Mix of dark and light (white) backgrounds per slide adds variety
