# Reference: Revolut / Finance App

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Real person portrait (no phone)**: Slide 2 is a woman's face portrait + star rating + quote — professional and human
- **Two phones side by side**: Slide 5 shows 2 phones of same size side by side (trading/crypto views)
- **Light lavender bg**: Slide 1 uses a soft `#c8c8f0` lavender background (unique, not typical dark or neon)
- **Stacked phones**: Slide 5 shows a larger phone + smaller phone stacked (top-right area)
- **Dark navy main slides**: Most feature slides use dark navy `#0f1123` background

## Color palette
- Hero slide bg: `#c0c0e8` (soft lavender)
- Feature slides bg: `#0f1123` (dark navy)
- Accent: gradient from blue `#3d6aff` to purple `#9b59b6`
- Text: `#FFFFFF` on dark, `#0f1123` on light

## Typography
- Headline: ~110px, bold, title case
- Copy: "Track Your Finances", "Real-Time Stocks", "Manage Account" — benefit-first
- Subtitle: Short descriptor, ~46px, muted

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Lavender bg + hand holding phone + "Track Your Finances" + phone screenshot visible
- Slide 2: Dark bg + woman portrait photo (no phone) + ★★★★★ + testimonial quote
- Slide 3: Dark bg + centered phone + "Manage Account" headline
- Slide 4: Dark bg + phone + floating menu cards overlaid on phone
- Slide 5: Dark bg + 2 phones (different sizes) + "Buy & Sell Crypto"

## Key takeaways
- Soft lavender as hero slide bg is unexpected and memorable in fintech (usually all-dark)
- Real person portrait slide (no phone) humanizes financial apps effectively
- Two phones side by side for a complex feature (like trading dashboard) communicates depth
- Keep benefit-oriented headlines for fintech ("Track Your Finances" not "Dashboard")
