# Reference: Dating App (Pink/Red Theme)

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Slide 1**: Full bleed couple photo + "Find Your Match" bold text + ★★★★★ + quote — no phone, just hero
- **Saturated pink/red background**: `#E83060` / `#FF2060` — bold, recognizable, emotional
- **Phone on colored bg**: Slides 2–5 — phone on solid pink bg, no dark mode at all
- **Floating avatar circles**: Slide 5 shows multiple circular profile avatars floating around the phone
- **Interest tags grid**: Slide 3 shows phone screen with interest selection tags (Reading, Photography, Gaming...) as a feature highlight

## Color palette
- Primary: `#E83060` (hot pink/red)
- Light hero: `#f9f0f3` (blush/cream for slide 1)
- Text on pink: `#FFFFFF`
- Accent within phone: `#E83060`

## Typography
- Headline: ~130px, bold, title case
- "Find Your Match", "Pick Your Favorite Passions", "Browse profiles & connect"
- Font: Bold grotesque

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Blush bg + couple photo (full bleed, left half) + "Find Your Match" right side + stars + quote
- Slide 2: Pink bg + phone centered + "Pick Your Favorite Passions"
- Slide 3: Pink bg + phone centered + 5 profile avatar circles floating top
- Slide 4: Pink bg + phone + spotlight on card swipe feature
- Slide 5: Pink bg + phone + floating profile circles around it + "Join the community & match"

## Key takeaways
- Floating circular avatars around phone = great for social/community apps
- Split layout (photo left, text right) for slide 1 works well without needing a device
- Hot pink / magenta is highly effective for romance/social apps — don't be afraid of saturation
- Stars + user quote in the slide 1 hero spot is the strongest social proof placement
