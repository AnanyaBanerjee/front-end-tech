# Reference: Fitness/Workout App (Yellow Theme — Hevy-style)

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Yellow as primary brand bg**: `#F5B800` / `#FFC107` fills slides — bold, energetic
- **Hero with awards**: "#1 Fitness App", "20M+ Users", "4.8 Rating" with wreath icons — strong credibility opening
- **Tilted phone on yellow bg**: Slide 1 — phone at an angle, workout library visible
- **Dark phone on yellow bg**: High contrast works because screenshots are dark-themed
- **"See Change" colored highlight**: One accent word in yellow against the headline for emphasis
- **White bg for contrast**: Slide 4 uses white/light bg to break the yellow monotony — smart

## Color palette
- Primary: `#F5B800` (golden yellow)
- Dark bg: `#1a1a1a`
- White accent: `#FFFFFF`
- Text on yellow: `#000000` (pure black)
- Text on dark: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Headlines: Bold, mixed case — "#1 Fitness App", "Build Custom Routines", "Reach Your Goals"
- Font: Heavy sans-serif
- Size: ~120px at 1320px canvas

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Yellow bg + phone at angle (left-center) + award badges (top-left) + "20M+ Users"
- Slide 2: Dark bg + phone + "Build Custom Routines" — yellow accent in headline
- Slide 3: Yellow bg + centered phone + pop-up UI card on top of phone + "Reach Your Goals"
- Slide 4: White bg + hand holding phone + "See Change" — light for contrast
- Slide 5: Yellow bg + 2 phones + "Join Fitness Group"

## Key takeaways
- Golden/mustard yellow works beautifully with dark screenshot content (high contrast)
- Award badges ("20M+ Users", "4.8 Rating") in the hero slide carry significant conversion weight
- Pop-up UI cards overlaid ON TOP of phone in slide 3 = shows feature depth in one composition
- White bg slide mid-set = visual reset that makes the yellow slides hit harder
- Hand holding phone (slide 4) provides natural, aspirational energy break from flat frames
