# Reference: urban.x / Sneaker Shopping App

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Different bg color per slide**: Brown → yellow → cream → green → grey — each slide has own identity
- **3D cartoon characters**: Illustrated 3D characters (boy with sneaker bag, woman shopping) beside the phone
- **QR code on hero slide**: `urban.x`, "Download Now!" + QR code — drives direct download
- **Rating badge**: "4.8 rating" in white pill bottom-left of hero slide
- **2 phones side by side**: Slide 5 — 2 phones at slight tilt, same size, side by side
- **Character interacting with phone**: 3D character appears to hand the phone to user or point at it

## Color palette
- Slide 1 bg: `#4A2C2A` (warm dark brown)
- Slide 2 bg: `#F5C842` (golden yellow)
- Slide 3 bg: `#F5EDD6` (warm cream)
- Slide 4 bg: `#1E3A2F` (dark forest green)
- Slide 5 bg: `#9B9B9B` (neutral grey)

## Typography
- Headline: Bold, large, left-aligned
- "Access real market data.", "Build your collection", "Shop with confidence"
- Font: Heavy grotesque, ~100px

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Brown bg + phone left + "Access real market data" + QR code + 4.8 rating badge
- Slide 2: Yellow bg + 3D character (boy with bags) + phone right + "Build your collection"
- Slide 3: Cream bg + 3D character (woman) + phone center + "Shop with confidence"
- Slide 4: Green bg + 2 phones showing product details + "Authentic every time"
- Slide 5: Grey bg + 2 tilted phones side by side + hashtag copy

## Key takeaways
- Different bg color per slide = maximum visual variety; feels like a full visual identity
- 3D characters beside phones communicate lifestyle and aspiration without real photos
- QR code on slide 1 is smart — users on App Store can directly scan to download
- Rating badge (4.8 ★) displayed as a graphic element (not just text) adds trust at a glance
- Warm earthy tones (brown, cream, forest green) work for fashion/sneaker category
