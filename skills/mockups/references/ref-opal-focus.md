# Reference: Opal (Screen Time / Focus App)

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Slide 1**: Bold product name + headline + phone partially shown + lifestyle photo bg (person lying down) — powerful visual
- **3D gem illustration**: Opal's gem logo as a 3D object inside the phone screen — stands out immediately
- **Headline pill/badge style**: Short bold text inside a rounded pill/label box overlaid on background photo ("Daily Focus Report", "Hold your attention.")
- **Massive typographic slide**: Slide 4 — no phone — just "2H30MIN YOU WERE SUPER FOCUSED TODAY" in massive all-caps white text on dramatic landscape photo background
- **Hand holding phone**: Slide 3 — a hand holds the phone (not a frame) — very natural/lifestyle feel
- **Confetti/celebration slide**: Slide 5 shows phone with confetti particles + "You did it!" — feature moment
- **Stars + testimonial**: Slide 2 — "★★★★★ This app saved me a lot of time. Love it!" — pure testimonial, no phone

## Color palette
- Background: `#0a0a0a` dark or `#1a2e1a` dark green
- Accent 1: `#CCFF47` (bright yellow-green)
- Accent 2: `rgba(204,255,71,0.15)` (muted pill bg)
- Text: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Font: Bold sans-serif, mix of ALL CAPS and sentence case
- Slide 4 mega headline: ~200px at 1320px canvas
- Feature pill text: ~40px, pill background
- Testimonial: ~70px quote text

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Dark bg + app name + "Master Screen Time" + partial phone bottom
- Slide 2: Dark bg + ★★★★★ + quote text — NO phone
- Slide 3: Teal/green bg + hand holding phone (photo asset, not CSS frame)
- Slide 4: Landscape nature photo bg + mega ALL CAPS headline overlay — NO phone
- Slide 5: Dark bg + phone with confetti + "You did it!" achievement

## Key takeaways
- Massive typographic slide (no phone) mid-set breaks monotony dramatically
- Testimonial slide (stars + quote, no phone) is one of the most effective trust slides
- Hand holding phone photo feels more human than a bare CSS frame
- Achievement/celebration moment as a slide is underused but very compelling
- Yellow-green on near-black is one of the most legible color combos in app stores
