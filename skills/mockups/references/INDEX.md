## Top 5 patterns to steal from every reference

1. **Slide 1 = brand poster** — Lifestyle photo or 3D character, NO feature copy, just claim + stars
2. **Alternating light/dark** — Never 3 dark slides in a row (Nike is the gold standard)
3. **Testimonial/proof slide** — Stars + quote with no phone (Opal, Nike, Revolut all use this)
4. **Press logos slide** — If the app has any media coverage, one slide = just the logos (Wellora)
5. **Award badges in slide 1** — "App of the Day", "10M+ Users", "4.8★" at the top of the hero
