# Reference: Claude AI App

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Warm earth tones**: Terracotta/rust `#C4602A` as brand color — unique, warm, stands out in the AI app category
- **Consistent colored background**: Same warm terracotta on every feature slide — strong brand identity
- **Two overlapping phones**: Slides 3–5 show 2–3 phones stacked/overlapping, showing multiple screens
- **Dark text on light elements**: White text on warm bg — readable and premium
- **Stars rating integration**: ★★★★★ rating displayed in hero slide as trust signal

## Color palette
- Primary: `#C4602A` (terracotta/rust)
- Background (hero): `#f5f0ea` (warm cream/off-white)
- Background (features): `#C4602A` (solid brand fill)
- Text: `#FFFFFF` on brand color, `#1a1a1a` on cream
- Accent on brand bg: `rgba(255,255,255,0.85)`

## Typography
- Font: Serif or humanist sans (editorial feel)
- Style: Title case, moderate weight — not all caps
- Headline: "Meet Claude, The next generation AI Assistant in the World"
- Feature headlines: "Write Better and Easier", "Organize Your Work", "Solve Problems"
- Size: ~100px headline at 1320px canvas

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Cream bg + large phone (slightly tilted) + headline left + star rating
- Slides 2–5: Terracotta bg + 1–2 phones + short feature headline top-left
- Slide 5: Terracotta bg + 2 overlapping phones + 3D robot character (personality)

## Key takeaways
- Terracotta is a memorable choice in a category full of blue/purple AI apps
- Consistent single brand color across all feature slides = strong cohesion
- Short editorial headlines ("Organize Your Work") outperform benefit-focused copy
- 3D character adds personality without needing custom illustration on every slide
