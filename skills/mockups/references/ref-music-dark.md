# Reference: Music App (Dark Purple/Pink Theme)

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Slide 1**: Full-bleed 3D avatar (girl with headphones) — NO phone. Just lifestyle art + big bold "FELL THE MUSIC VIBE" at bottom
- **3D floating icons**: Music note emojis scattered at random rotations around the phone (3–4 icons, 50–60% opacity)
- **Textured background**: Dark bg with subtle dot/noise pattern texture (not flat black)
- **Perspective-tilted phone**: Phone shown at a slight angle in slide 2
- **Multiple phone sizes**: One slide shows a large phone + small phone side-by-side for depth

## Color palette
- Background: `#1a0a24` (very dark purple)
- Accent 1: `#C848FF` (purple/magenta)
- Accent 2: `#FF4EAA` (hot pink)
- Text: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Font: Bold grotesque, all caps for main headline
- Size: ~140px at 1320px canvas
- Line-height: ~0.95

## Layout pattern
- Slide 1: Lifestyle art → text at bottom third → no phone
- Slide 2: Dark bg → phone tilted → text at top left
- Slide 3: Feature slide → centered phone + scattered music icons

## Key takeaways
- Lifestyle illustration/3D character as hero (no phone) is very effective for emotional branding
- Scatter floating themed icons (emoji or 3D) around the phone — adds playfulness without effort
- Dark purple backgrounds feel premium and genre-appropriate
- "FELL THE MUSIC VIBE" breaks grammar intentionally — works for energy brand
