# Reference: Nike Training Club

Source: App Store screenshot set, shared 2026-04-23

## What this set does well

- **Alternating light/dark**: Lime green bg → dark bg with phone → lime green → dark with phone — perfect rhythm
- **Solid brand color as background**: `#BFFF00` / `#C8FF00` (Nike lime) fills the entire slide — phone sits on top
- **Full bleed athlete photo**: Slide 1 and 2 are just photos with text overlay — no device frame
- **Consistent font weight**: All headlines are heavy/black weight
- **Dark text on light bg**: Black text on lime green, white text on dark bg

## Color palette
- Primary: `#BFFF00` or `#C8FF00` (lime green)
- Dark: `#1a1a1a`
- Dark green: `#1a2e0f`
- Text on light: `#000000`
- Text on dark: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Font: Bold sans-serif, title case
- Headline: ~130px at 1320px canvas
- Body: ~50px
- Style: Short punchy lines — "ANYTIME ANYWHERE", "FROM YOGA TO STRENGTH TO RUNNING"

## Layout patterns
- Slide 1: Full bleed athlete photo — no phone — "ANYTIME ANYWHERE" bold text bottom-left
- Slide 2: Full bleed photo + phone inset in bottom-right corner
- Slide 3: Lime bg + phone centered + "ACCESS THOUSANDS OF WORKOUTS" top
- Slide 4: Dark green bg + phone at slight angle + "AT THE GYM"
- Slide 5: Lime bg + person silhouette photo bg + phone overlay

## Key takeaways
- Lime/neon yellow-green on black = instantly recognizable, high energy
- Full bleed lifestyle photo for slides 1–2 (no phone) sets the tone before feature slides
- Alternating light/dark is the gold standard rhythm for a 5–6 slide set
- Short ALL CAPS hero text dominates — subtitle is optional at thumbnail scale
