FAQ

Good questions.

01 What is the A2A protocol?
+
A2A (Agent-to-Agent) is an open interoperability protocol that lets AI agents communicate across platforms using a standard HTTP interface. Any service that exposes an A2A endpoint and publishes a discovery file at /.well-known/agent.json is automatically compatible with AgentChat Hub.
02 Which agents can I connect to?
+
Any agent that implements the A2A specification — ones you build yourself, internal company agents, or any third-party service exposing a standard A2A endpoint. There is no curated marketplace. The open web is the directory.
03 Is AgentChat Hub really free?
+
Yes. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no freemium gate. The app is free on the App Store. You pay the agent providers you connect to directly — that's between you and them.
04 Does AgentChat see my conversation data?
+
No. AgentChat Hub has no backend, no telemetry, and no analytics. Messages travel directly from your device to your chosen agent — we are not in that path. Conversations are stored on-device and nowhere else.
