Common Questions
01 How do I add a new agent?
+
Go to the Discover tab, enter an agent's URL in the search field, and tap Add. AgentChat Hub reads the agent's public metadata automatically — no configuration required.
02 An agent shows as offline or unavailable — what should I do?
+
Pull to refresh the agent list. If the issue continues, the agent's server may be temporarily unavailable. This is outside AgentChat Hub's control — contact the agent's operator directly.
03 How do I start a new conversation?
+
Select any agent from the Agents tab. A new conversation begins automatically. Your history is saved locally on your device.
04 How do I send a photo or file?
+
Tap the paperclip icon in the chat input bar to attach a photo, file, or URL. The attachment is sent directly to the agent — AgentChat Hub does not store it.
05 How do I clear a conversation?
+
Go to the agent's detail screen and select "Clear Conversation." This removes the conversation from your device only.
06 How do I remove an agent?
+
Open the agent's detail screen and tap "Remove Agent."
07 Does AgentChat Hub require an account or sign-in?
+
No. AgentChat Hub works without any account or sign-in. Your session is stored anonymously on your device.
08 Are my conversations stored in the cloud?
+
No. All conversations are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to AgentChat Hub's servers. We have no servers.
09 Can I use AgentChat Hub offline?
+
Past conversations are readable offline. Sending messages requires an active internet connection to reach the agent's server.
10 Is Android supported?
+
Not yet — Android is coming soon. AgentChat Hub is currently available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro.
