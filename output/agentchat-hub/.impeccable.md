## Design Context

### Users
Everyone who uses technology — builders, engineers, PMs, hobbyists, and anyone curious about AI agents. Broad audience, not developer-exclusive. Users discover the app and want to trust it immediately.

### Brand Personality
Open, trustworthy, inviting. AgentChat Hub is free for everyone, collects no data, stores no info, sells no ads. The brand should feel like a public utility — reliable, accessible, no strings attached.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode — trust, clarity, broad daytime browsing audience
- **Palette**: Warm off-white base (#F8F7F4), deep teal accent (#0D7C6E) — NOT the AI purple/blue palette
- **Typography**: Bricolage Grotesque (display) + Figtree (body) + JetBrains Mono (technical)
- **Tone**: Warm, confident, human. Not cold-tech. Not startup-hype. Like a well-designed public service.
- **Anti-references**: Dark mode AI dashboards, purple neon gradients, glassmorphism-heavy SaaS pages

### Design Principles
1. **Trust through clarity** — Clean layouts, readable type, no visual noise
2. **Everyone means everyone** — Accessible, warm, not intimidating or developer-gatekept
3. **Show, don't sell** — Let the product speak through a realistic chat mockup, not marketing copy
4. **Openness as identity** — The A2A open protocol ethos should permeate the visual language
5. **One accent, maximum impact** — Single teal accent used sparingly for emphasis
