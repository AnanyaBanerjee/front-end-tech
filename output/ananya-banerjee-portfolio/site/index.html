About

Engineer by craft.
Builder by instinct.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at LinkedIn in the San Francisco Bay Area, where I work on systems that reach hundreds of millions of people. Before that, I won two back-to-back hackathons at eBay — building Generative AI products that judges called groundbreaking.

I hold an MS in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas (Master's Research Fellow, Jonsson Scholarship) and a BE from BITS Pilani. My research spans NLP, computer vision, and knowledge-infused deep learning.

Outside of my day job, I build and ship iOS apps, design AI agents, and write for Towards Data Science, Towards AI, Analytics Vidhya, and The Startup. I'm Bengali, fluent in four languages, and deeply curious about the intersection of technology and human experience.
