Quick help

Getting Started

Tap the + button on the Home screen to add your first moment. Choose a category, write what happened, and tap Save. That is it.

Your Privacy

ItMadeMyDay collects no data at all. Everything stays on your device. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policy

Export & Import

Go to Settings and tap Export to save all your moments as a JSON file. You can import this file back at any time to restore your vault.

Common questions

Tap the + button on the Home screen. Select a category (Compliment, Memory, Heart-to-Heart, Quote, Link, or Photo), fill in the details, and tap Save. Your moment is added to your vault immediately.

Go to the Collections tab and tap New Collection. Give it a name, then start adding moments to it. You can add any saved moment to a Collection by opening it and tapping Add to Collection.

Deleted moments cannot be recovered once removed, as ItMadeMyDay stores all data locally on your device with no server backup. We recommend exporting your vault regularly from Settings to keep a JSON backup.

Go to Settings in the app and tap Export Moments. Your vault will be saved as a JSON file that you can store in Files, share via AirDrop, or email to yourself. To restore, tap Import Moments in Settings and select the file.
