Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

ItMadeMyDay is operated by Ananya Banerjee ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the ItMadeMyDay iOS app and this website.

1. What Data We Collect

ItMadeMyDay collects no data. The app does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal information. All content you save — moments, photos, quotes, links, and any other entries — is stored exclusively on your device using local device storage.

No data ever leaves your device. There is no server-side component to the ItMadeMyDay app.

2. How We Use Your Data

We do not collect any data, so we do not use any. There is no server, no database, and no analytics system associated with the ItMadeMyDay app.

3. Third-Party Services

ItMadeMyDay does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-processing services within the app.

This website (the landing page you are reading) does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools of any kind.

4. Cookies

The ItMadeMyDay app does not use cookies. This website does not set any cookies on your browser.

5. Data Retention

Since we collect no data, there is no data retention policy applicable to us. Your moments stay on your device until you delete them within the app or uninstall the app. You can export and delete your data at any time from the Settings screen.

6. Your Rights (GDPR — EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your personal data.

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you exercise these rights directly within the app. You can view, edit, export, or delete all your moments from within ItMadeMyDay at any time. No request to us is required.

For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us at .

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) may provide you additional rights regarding your personal information.

You have the right to know what personal information we collect (none), the right to delete your personal information (deletable directly in the app), and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell any data, ever).

8. Children's Privacy

ItMadeMyDay is rated 4+ on the App Store and is designed to be suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or under any applicable age threshold in your jurisdiction — and we do not collect personal information from anyone at all.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
