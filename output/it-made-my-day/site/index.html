Good questions.

ItMadeMyDay is for the hard days. It's a private vault where you save the moments that made you smile, feel seen, feel proud, or feel loved — so that when life gets difficult, you can open the app and remember who you are and why it's worth it. It's not a journal. It's not a photo album. It's a happiness reserve you build every day, for the days when you need it most.

Yes. ItMadeMyDay is free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no premium tiers. Everything is included from the start.

No. ItMadeMyDay collects zero personal data. All moments you save are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing is transmitted to any server, anywhere, ever.

You can save six types of moments: Compliments (kind things people said to you), Memories (experiences worth keeping), Heart-to-Hearts (meaningful conversations), Quotes (words that moved you), Links (articles, videos, pages you loved), and Photos.

Boost is the heart of the app. You tell it how you're feeling — Sad, Anxious, Celebrating, Lonely, Grateful, or a custom mood you created — and it surfaces the saved moments most likely to help. It's not random. It's the app reaching into your vault and saying: here's proof that things have been good, and they will be again.

Collections are groups of moments you create and organize yourself. The app includes examples like Kind Words, Little Wins, and Happy Memories — but you can create any collection with a custom name and color. Add moments to multiple collections, and browse them separately anytime.

Yes. Go to Settings → Export My Moments to save a file with all your moments, collections, and settings. You can import that file back using Settings → Import Moments — restoring everything exactly as it was, or transferring it to a new device.

Yes. Settings → Delete All Data permanently removes all your moments, collections, and settings from the device. This cannot be undone, so the app asks you to confirm before proceeding. Since there is no server, the deletion is complete and instant.
