Your moments stay yours

ItMadeMyDay was built with a single rule: your personal moments belong to you and no one else. There is no server, no database, no account, and no data collection of any kind.

No Account Required Open the app and start saving. No sign-up, no email, no password.
No Cloud Sync All data lives on your device. Nothing is sent to any server, ever.
Zero Data Collection No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry — not even anonymous usage stats.
You Own Your Data Export your moments as JSON anytime from Settings. Import them back whenever you like.

Common questions

Yes. ItMadeMyDay is free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no premium tiers. Everything is included from the start.

No. ItMadeMyDay collects zero personal data. All moments you save are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing is transmitted to any server, anywhere, ever.

You can save six types of moments: Compliments (kind things people said to you), Memories (experiences worth keeping), Heart-to-Hearts (meaningful conversations), Quotes (words that moved you), Links (articles, videos, pages you loved), and Photos.

Boost lets you browse your saved moments filtered by how you are currently feeling — Sad, Anxious, Lonely, Celebrating, or Grateful. It surfaces the moments most likely to help, comfort, or resonate with you in that moment.

Collections are curated groups of moments that you organize yourself. You can create any Collection you like — a set of your favorite compliments, memories from a particular trip, or quotes from a book — and add moments to it for easy browsing later.

Yes. You can export all your moments as a JSON file from the Settings screen at any time. You can also import that file back into the app to restore your vault, or transfer it to a new device.
