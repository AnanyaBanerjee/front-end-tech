## Design Context

### Users
People who want to remember the small joys of daily life — compliments they received, meaningful conversations, funny moments, things that made them smile. Primarily used alone, in quiet moments, often on a phone. Emotional, personal, reflective context.

### Brand Personality
Warm · Personal · Gentle

The app is like a best friend who helps you remember what matters. Not clinical, not performative. No productivity vibes — pure personal warmth.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm cream/parchment backgrounds (#FBF8F3), amber accent color from the app (#F5A623), Bricolage Grotesque for headings (characterful, slightly condensed), Nunito for body (rounded, approachable). Light mode — this is a daytime, feel-good app. Minimalist layout with generous whitespace. Phone mockups presented with gentle tilts in the hero. Dark section for screenshots contrast.

Anti-references: No dark-mode SaaS dashboards. No startup-y neon. No "productivity app" vibes. No generic motivational poster aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. Every element should feel human — no corporate coldness
2. Privacy is a feature — celebrate it visually (the "0 bytes" stat)
3. Let the screenshots speak — the app is beautiful, put phones front and center
4. Warmth over polish — slightly imperfect compositions beat rigid grids
5. Copy is factual and direct — no "revolutionary", no "seamless", no marketing speak
